About Our School

School of Prabhat was founded with a vision to create leaders, thinkers, and compassionate human beings. With state-of-the-art infrastructure, sports facilities, science labs, and a vibrant arts program, we nurture every child’s potential. Our student-teacher ratio is 15:1, ensuring personalized attention.

We believe education goes beyond textbooks — we foster curiosity, creativity, and resilience through experiential learning, field trips, and community projects.

Dr. Anita Sharma

Principal, School of Prabhat

“Every child is unique, and our mission is to provide a nurturing ground where dreams take flight.”
